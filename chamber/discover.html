City of Irvine

Irvine is a city in Orange County, California. The city of Irvine is a great place to live, for more than 40 years, residents and businesses have been choosing the City of Irvine because of its dedication to maintaining its reputation as one of the safest, master-planned, business-friendly communities in the country.

Giant Wheel at Irvine Spectrum Center

The giant Ferris Wheel is situaded in the center of the shopping/entertainment district of Irvine, this attraction is 108 feet tall and custom designed with 52,00 energy-efficient LED lights. The colorful lights make the Wheel mesmerizing even from the ground.
