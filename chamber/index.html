Site Name

Irvine Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Irvine chamber of commerce website has as purpose to promote and support the community, business and tourism through marketing, promotion, and events, in order to make the greater Irvine Area an amazing place to live, work and play. In this website the members will found deals and discounts from other chamber members, business listing in a member directory, and a variety of other programs and services designed to promote business activity in Irvine.
