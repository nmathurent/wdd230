Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Contrast is an impactful element that is used in many realms of design. Not only does strong contrast create an attention-grabbing visual, but it also allows design elements to work well together despite their differences and creates a sense of balance.

White Space

Squarespace

squarespace.com

The white space rule in design recommends using enough blank spaces in a design layout. It means maintaining the spaces between lines of text, paragraphs, and different design elements.

Rule of Thirds

Breitling

breitling.com

Breitling uses rule of thirds to draw immediate attention to its products, the watches. On the homepage the watch is placed on the right thirds section of the photo.
