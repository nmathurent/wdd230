Persona 1

Name: Entrepeneur Mark
Background: Mark has time and money to spend and wants to invest in a Business.
Demographics: He is in his 40’s, makes $75,000-$90,000 a year and lives in the Southwest United States.
Identifiers: He is young, passionate, motivated and optimistic, and he relays on Google to find information.
Goals: Mark tried in the past to create a business in another state (Texas) but he could not initiate it and moved to California last year. He wants help figuring out how to start a business in Orange County area.
Challenges: He is unsure of who to trust but is optimistic about finding the right information about his line of business.
Scenario:
Can I find information about my type of business in Irvine?
My website can provide information on his line of businesses, can educate him about new techniques and new technology through seminars.
